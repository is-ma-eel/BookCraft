Book Binding

Book binding is the process of putting sheets of paper together in a protective cover by stitching, gluing, clipping and the like. It can also be achieved through mechanical means.

In book binding, the following activities are undertaken:

  • Cutting of sheets
  • Collating of sheets
  • Fastening of sheets and cover together
  • Gluing and stitching
  • Trimming the book
  • Designing the cover
