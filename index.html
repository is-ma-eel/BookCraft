Overview

Book craft deals with the production of books and repairing of damage books. The production of books deals with teh collation and fastening of loose or folded sheets (folio) together into a protective cover. This is termed as bookbinding.

It also deals with the designing of covers and creating items like envelops and other packages from cards or paper.

Book

A book is the collation of loose or folded sheets fastened together in a designed cover. Books come in different forms for different purpose. The main reason why books are bound in different ways is to suit its purpose. For example, note books are bound with the multi-section binding techniques because it has more than one signature and needs to be opened flat.
